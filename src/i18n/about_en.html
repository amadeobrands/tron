Cryptomeetup is a decentralized events platform powered by cutting-edge blockchain technology. Users can publish activities on the 3D virtual earth, and they can also buy and sell land assets to campaign for land administrators.We hope to combine blockchain games with activities to create a more sustainable developing game model.

In the Tron version,we will firstly release land trading function. On the 3D interface, we will provide 220 countries, which are unique NFT token and the assets on public chain Tron.

The initial price of each country is 10TRX. By transaction, the corresponding country's price will automatically rise. The price fluctuation will follow the rules below:

last transaction price Puchase price Dividends for developers Jackpot
<20 trx Price * 200 / 95 5% 2%
20- 500 trx Price* 135 / 96 4% 2%
500-2000 trx Price * 125 / 97  3% 2%
2000-5000 trx Price * 117 / 97 3% 2%
>=5000 trx Price * 115 / 98 2% 2%

The countdown of the jackpool is one month. For every 100 TRX used to buy a country, the countdown is added 60 seconds. At the end of the countdown, the last purchaser of the game will receive all the prizes.

When the countdown is finished, the next round will be opened automatically. At the beginning of each round, the owner of the country is remaining the owner of the country at the end of the previous round. But the countdown and the starting price of the country will be reset.
